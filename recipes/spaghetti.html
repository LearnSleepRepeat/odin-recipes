Spaghetti

Here i will show you how to make the best spaghetti you've ever had.

Out of this world

Did you know that pasta was invented in China? Well, now you know and now your Italian friends will also stop talking to you.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (dried)
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps to cook this incredible recipe

  1. Put pan on stove, add lots of water
  2. Add salt to water
  3. Put spaghetti into the water when it's boiling
  4. boil for amount of minutes that the pasta needs until it's al dente
  5. finito e buon appetito
