Delicious Tofuscramble

Here i will show you how to make an easy tofu omelette, also known as tofu scramble (or not).

Looks yummi, right?

So, i was wandering in the himalayas one day, and i was looking at this old sherpa that was carying a yap. Did you know that yaks can weigh up to 800 kg? Anyway... blabla bla

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • cooking oil
  • smokey tofu

Steps to cook

  1. cut tofu into small pieces
  2. heat up oil (close to smoking), add tofu and cook until crispy & golden brown
  3. spice with paprika, salt & pepper
  4. quirl the eggs, add salt & pepper, the tofu and make an omelette
